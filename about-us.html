WHO ARE WE?

Astronomy Club - VIT Stellar, where passion meets the cosmos! Founded by astronomy enthusiasts at
Vellore Institute of Technology, our club is dedicated to exploring the wonders of the universe and fostering
a community of stargazers, scientists, and dreamers. Our motive is to ignite curiosity and inspire exploration
of the cosmos. Through engaging workshops, star parties, and informative sessions, we aim to make
astronomy accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from beginners to seasoned astronomers.
